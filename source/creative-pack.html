Video slate

Full scripts, shot lists and the UGC creator brief are in the companion doc: csp-cyber-safe-curriculum-video-ugc-scripts.md

V1. "POV: Sunday night"

Teacher talking head, 20–25s. The Sunday-prep pain, resolved. 3 hook variations to test. Cold.

V2. Educator Hub walkthrough

Screen capture, ~20s. Shows what "ready-to-teach" looks like. No face needed. Consideration.

V3. Teacher UGC

Authentic creator testimonial, 30–45s. Highest-performing format for this audience. Cold.

V4. Faceless explainer

HeyGen, ~15s. Fast faceless variant to test against the talking head. Optional.

QA summary Pass with flags

Compliance QA (education): No blocks. Regimes: Australian Consumer Law, education marketing, testimonial rules.

  • Flag: confirm "eSafety endorsed" reflects current, accurate wording before publishing.
  • Flag: testimonial quotes and the "reduction in incident reports" claim need substantiation and permission (Data QA), and must be representative.
  • Fix applied: tone kept on workload, curriculum and confidence. No fear-based framing, no children depicted.
  • Note: "free trial" must be genuinely free with terms clear on the landing page.

Copywriter QA: Pass. Native voice, no AI tells, hooks lead, one idea per creative.

Brand QA: Pass. Creatives are deliberately CSP-native, not Hedgehog-branded. Agency branding confined to this internal pack. Australian English throughout.

This review identifies known risk patterns and is not legal advice.
